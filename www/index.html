Empower Choice through Digital Zen.

The first walled-garden shopping experience for kids with Special Educational Needs. Creating a safe space for independence.

The Problem

Standard shopping is overwhelming.

Traditional e-commerce is filled with noise: predatory patterns, overstimulating animations, and complex navigation. For kids with SEND, this makes choosing a simple gift an impossible task.

Parent-Curated Lists

Carers select real-world items and experiences, building a distraction-free list tailored specifically to the child's interests.

Simplified Choice

A "Child View" mode replaces complex UIs with pure, binary choices. Green for "This one," Red for "Not this time."

Zen Environment

Zero advertisements, zero auto-play video, and zero predatory UI. Just a calm, neutral space to communicate desires.
